# Theology of technology stewardship

> Internal standard for Lake Shore Church platform documentation, blueprints, and PDFs. Congregation-facing copy: `/dedication` on the public site.

## Director of Technology

**The Holy Spirit is named as Director of Technology** — God Himself guiding every technical decision, ethic, and purpose for digital ministry. This is stated explicitly and professionally, not metaphorically casual.

**Human technology steward** (system role: `admin`) — the person who operates deployments, Supabase, Sanity, and security day to day. This role is **operational**, not sovereign. Wording in docs and reports:

- Prefer: *technology steward*, *human technology steward*, *technology administrator (human steward)*
- Avoid implying a person *is* the ultimate director of technology without the Holy Spirit clause

## Approved phrasing (reports & blueprints)

| Context | Wording |
|--------|---------|
| Cover / footer | *Under the direction of the Holy Spirit, Director of Technology* |
| Prepared by | *Prepared for Lake Shore Church · Holy Spirit, Director of Technology · Human technology steward* |
| Auth tier `admin` | *Technology administrator (human steward)* — serves under the Holy Spirit as Director of Technology |
| Leadership pitch | *Primary investment is the technology steward's time, serving under the Holy Spirit's direction* |
